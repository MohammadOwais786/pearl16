About Us

Clear your calendar from March 17th to the 20th for a fully immersive cultural experience guaranteed to delight all the senses! Pearl, first inaugurated in 2009, has now established itself as one of the leading cultural festivals in the country, where people from all across converge in a celebration of talent.

BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus prides itself, every year, on hosting some of the most prestigious cultural competitions in India. Among this year's lineup of contests are Till Deaf Do We Part, the gritty battle of the bands, Gliteratti, the fashion showcase , Terpsichore, for college dance troupes , Catharsis, our film festival and Fraglore, the 3-day event dedicated solely to gaming. Pearl also boasts a wide variety of photography, arts, quizzing, theatre, and literary events this year.

So join us in yet another edition of the cultural bonanza we know and adore, this year inspired by the theme "Live Evil". In March, something wicked this way comes!

Professional Shows

This Pearl, prepare to be amazed by the roster of pro-shows we have ready for you. Be swayed by the melodic multi genre notes of Thaikkudam Bridge, and keep your moves ready for the EDM beats of Nucleya. Abish Matthew will also be here to leave you rolling on the floor with his wise-cracks. And last but not the least, get ready to be ‘rocked’ by the actor-director-writer-singer, the multi-talented Farhan Akhtar. We hope to see you here too, to pump up the energy and have the time of your lives!

Till Deaf Do We Part

Because the statement 'Music is life' is a banality. Another of our Headliner events, Till Deaf Do We Part is Pearl's National Semi-Professional Indie Band Competition. Regional rounds were held at Bengaluru, Delhi and Mumbai. Winners from the regionals will battle it out during Pearl 2016 at BITS-Pilani Hyderabad.

Glitterati

Beauty and looks do matter. Do you think that you have the charm that woos people? Here's your chance to show-off your looks and win prizes for it. Our Headliner event, Glitterati, is THE fashion parade of the city of Pearls. Bring your swagger on the ramp and win the day, this Pearl.Dress up already?

Varuna Jitesh, the renowned designer for traditional wear, is all set to showcase her latest collections at Pearl 2016, BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus.


Terpsichore

If there is anything better than a dancer, it's a team of dancers battling it out to outshine each other! Presenting Terpsichore, the group dance competition where the best of college teams across India give it their all to win the big prize!
